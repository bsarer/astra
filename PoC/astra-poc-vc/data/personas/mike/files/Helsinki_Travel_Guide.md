# Helsinki Quick Guide — March 2026
**Trip dates:** March 14–17 | Hotel: Hotel Kämp

## Getting Around
- **HSL app** — download before arrival, covers metro, tram, bus
- Airport to city: Train I or P from Helsinki Airport (30 min, €5.50)
- Hotel Kämp is central — most attractions walkable

## Must-Do (Mike's interests: photography, craft beer, outdoors)

### Photography Spots
- **Oodi Central Library** — stunning white architecture, great interior shots
- **Senate Square at dawn** — cathedral reflection, almost no tourists early
- **Allas Sea Pool** — industrial port meets sauna culture, golden hour is magic
- **Kallio neighborhood** — street art, gritty urban texture
- **Suomenlinna** — fortress walls, sea views, historic cannons

### Craft Beer
- **Bryggeri Helsinki** — best local brewery, in a converted 1800s building
- **Pien** — tiny bar, rotating taps, locals only vibe
- **Teerenpeli** — Finnish whisky AND craft beer, Senate Square area

### Northern Lights (March 15 tour)
- Tour pickup: Hotel Kämp lobby at 8:00 PM
- Dress for -8°C: thermal base layer + fleece + waterproof jacket + hat + gloves
- Camera tips: manual mode, ISO 800–3200, f/2.8, 10–25 sec exposure, tripod essential
- Extra batteries — cold kills battery life fast, keep spares in inner pocket

## Food
- **Ravintola Savoy** — classic Finnish fine dining, expense it
- **Löyly** — sauna + restaurant on the waterfront (book ahead)
- **Market Square (Kauppatori)** — salmon soup, reindeer, local pastries
- **Fazer Café** — best Finnish cinnamon rolls and coffee

## Finnish Sauna Etiquette
- Nudity is normal and expected in public saunas
- Löyly has mixed-gender swimwear sauna if preferred
- Löyly Sauna booking: +358 9 6128 6550
